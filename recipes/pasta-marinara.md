# Pasta Marinara

## Ingredients

- [ ] $pasta = 8oz$
- [ ] $tomato_sauce = 2cups$
- [ ] $garlic = 3cloves$
- [ ] $olive_oil = 2tbsp$
- [ ] $basil = 1tbsp$
- [ ] $salt = 1tsp$

## Steps

1. Boil water in a large pot
2. Add $pasta$ to boiling water and cook for 8-10 minutes
3. Heat $olive_oil$ in a pan over medium heat
4. Add minced $garlic$ and cook for 1 minute
5. Add $tomato_sauce$, $basil$, and $salt$
6. Simmer for 10 minutes
7. Drain pasta and mix with sauce

## Nutrition Info

* 15g fat
* 80g carbs
* 12g protein
