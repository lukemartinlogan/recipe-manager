# Margherita Pizza Dough

[recipe_icon](margherita-pizza-dough.jpg)

## Ingredients
### Liquid Base
- [ ] $lukewarm_water = 1 1/2 cups, about 110Â°F$
- [ ] $instant_dry_yeast = 2 packets, 1/4 ounce each$
- [ ] $granulated_sugar = 2 tsp$

### Oil and Seasoning
- [ ] $olive_oil = 1/4 cup, plus more for bowl and drizzling$
- [ ] $coarse_salt = 1 tsp$

### Flour
- [ ] $all_purpose_flour = 4 cups, spooned and leveled, plus more for work surface$

## Instructions
1. Pour the $lukewarm_water$ into a large bowl; sprinkle with $instant_dry_yeast$ and let stand until foamy, about 5 minutes
2. Whisk in the $granulated_sugar$, $olive_oil$, and $coarse_salt$
3. Add $all_purpose_flour$ and stir until a sticky dough forms
4. Lightly brush another large bowl with a little $olive_oil$. Place dough inside and drizzle a little oil over the top
5. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside in a warm, draft-free place until dough has doubled in size, about 1 hour
6. Turn out onto a lightly floured work surface and gently knead 2 to 3 times, forming into a round. Let rest for about 10 more minutes
7. Use right away or store as is for 1 large pizza, cut in half for 2 medium pizzas, or divide into 4 equal portions for individual pizzas
