# Margherita Pizza

[recipe_icon](margherita-pizza.jpg)

## Ingredients
### Base
- [ ] $pizza_dough = 1/4 recipe, homemade pizza crust$
- [ ] $olive_oil = 2 tsp, extra virgin$
- [ ] $marinara_sauce = 2 tbsp$

### Aromatics
- [ ] $garlic = 1 clove, minced$

### Toppings
- [ ] $fresh_mozzarella = 3 oz, sliced into 1/4-inch thick pieces$
- [ ] $roma_tomato = 1, sliced$
- [ ] $basil_leaves = 2 large, sliced into ribbons$
- [ ] $black_pepper = freshly ground$

## Instructions
1. Place a pizza stone in the oven. Preheat the oven to 500Â°F, allowing the stone to get hot
2. Shape the $pizza_dough$ on a floured surface into an 8-inch round and 1/4-inch thick. Transfer it to a pizza peel lined with parchment paper. Reshape if necessary
3. Gently brush the dough with the $olive_oil$, then evenly spread the $marinara_sauce$ on, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge
4. Sprinkle the $garlic$ over the top. Top with the $fresh_mozzarella$ slices and $roma_tomato$, leaving space in between
5. Slide the parchment with the crust onto the heated stone. Bake for 8-10 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted and bubbly
6. Remove from oven and sprinkle the $basil_leaves$ over the top, along with 1-2 grinds of fresh $black_pepper$
7. Cut into wedges and serve immediately
