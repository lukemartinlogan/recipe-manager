# Spaghetti Squash

## Ingredients
- Spaghetti squash
- Salt
- Spaghetti sauce

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F
2. Cut the squash in half
3. Take out the seeds
4. Rinse the inside, but don't dry
5. Lightly salt
6. Cook for 45 minutes
7. After taking out of the oven – it should look like spaghetti
