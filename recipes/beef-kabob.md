# Beef Kabob

## Ingredients
- ⅓ cup soy sauce
- ½ cup olive oil
- ⅓ cup fresh lemon juice (2 lemons)
- ¼ cup worcestershire sauce
- 6tsp minced garlic
- 3tbsp dried basil
- 1tsp black pepper
- 1tsp red chilli paste
- 2lbs of beef cubes

## Directions
1. Combine all in a bowl
2. Preheat to 400°F and cook for about 20min
