# Chicken Sandwich

## Ingredients

- [ ] $chicken = 1/4lb$ 
- [ ] $black_pepper = 1tsp$
- [ ] $liquid_smoke = 1/2tsp$ 

## Steps

1. Put $black_pepper$ and $liquid_smoke$ on chicken
2. Heat up stove to medium-high
3. Put chicken on stove and char in olive oil
4. Put chicken in oven for 15 minutes

## Nutrition Info

* 10g fat
* 50g carbs
