# Chicken Sandwich

## Ingredients

* $chicken = 1/4lb$ 
* $black_pepper = 1tsp$
* $liquid_smoke = 1/2tsp$ 

## Steps

* Put $black_pepper$ and $liquid_smoke$ on chicken
* Heat up stove to medium-high
* Put chicken on stove and char in olive oil
* Put chicken in oven for 15 minutes

## Nutrition Info

* 10g fat
* 50g carbs
