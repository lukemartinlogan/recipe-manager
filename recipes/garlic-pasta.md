# Garlic Pasta

## Ingredients
- Whole wheat pasta
- Olive oil
- Minced Garlic
- Cayenne pepper
- Chili pepper
- Oregano
- Parsley
- Salt + black pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta according to package instructions
2. In a pan, put olive oil + garlic and cook until garlic browned.
3. Turn off heat.
4. Add (4-8)
5. Drain spaghetti and pour garlic mixture over
