# Hawaiian Chicken

## Ingredients
### Protein
- [ ] $chicken_thighs = 4 large$

### Oil
- [ ] $vegetable_oil = 2 tbsp$

### Marinade/Sauce
- [ ] $pineapple_juice = 1/2 cup$
- [ ] $soy_sauce = 1/2 cup, low-sodium$
- [ ] $light_brown_sugar = 1/2 cup, packed$
- [ ] $rice_vinegar = 1/4 cup$
- [ ] $fresh_lime_juice = 1 tbsp$

### Aromatics
- [ ] $garlic = 1 tbsp, finely minced$
- [ ] $fresh_ginger = 2 tsp, finely minced$

## Instructions
1. Prepare the marinade: In a large mixing bowl, combine the pineapple juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, vegetable oil, lime juice, garlic, and ginger. Mix until the sugar is fully dissolved.
