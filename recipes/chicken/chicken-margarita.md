# Margarita Chicken

## Ingredients
### Protein
- [ ] $chicken_breast = 3 lbs$

### Marinade
- [ ] $tequila = 1/2 cup$
- [ ] $olive_oil = 3 tbsp$
- [ ] $lime = 2, zested and juiced$
- [ ] $orange = 1 large, juiced$
- [ ] $agave_nectar = 2 tbsp$
- [ ] $minced_garlic = 1 tsp$

### Spices
- [ ] $chili_powder = 1 tsp$
- [ ] $cumin = 1 tsp$
- [ ] $oregano = 1 tsp$
- [ ] $salt = 1 tsp$
- [ ] $black_pepper = to taste$

### Optional
- [ ] $cilantro = 3-4 tbsp$

## Instructions
1. If very thick, slice $chicken_breast$ in half through the middle to create 2 thinner cutlets and place them in a gallon-size resealable plastic food storage bag
2. Combine $tequila$, $olive_oil$, $lime$, $orange$, $agave_nectar$, $minced_garlic$, $chili_powder$, $cumin$, $oregano$, $salt$, and $black_pepper$, and pour it over the $chicken_breast$
3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 3 and up to 8 hours
4. Grill the $chicken_breast$ over MEDIUM heat until cooked well throughout, approximately 6 to 8 minutes per side
5. Slice the chicken and sprinkle with $cilantro$. Serve as desired with lime wedges on the side
