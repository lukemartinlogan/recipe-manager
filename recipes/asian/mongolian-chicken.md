# Mongolian Chicken

## Ingredients
### Protein
- [ ] $chicken_breast = 1 1/4 lbs, thinly sliced$

### Coating and Oil
- [ ] $cornstarch = 1/4 cup + 2 tsp, divided use$
- [ ] $vegetable_oil = 3 tbsp$

### Aromatics
- [ ] $minced_garlic = 1 1/2 tsp$
- [ ] $minced_ginger = 1 tsp$
- [ ] $sesame_oil = 1 tsp, toasted$

### Sauce
- [ ] $soy_sauce = 1/2 cup, low sodium$
- [ ] $water = 1/3 cup$
- [ ] $brown_sugar = 1/2 cup, dark$

### Garnish
- [ ] $green_onions = 1/2 cup, cut into 1 inch pieces$
- [ ] $salt = to taste$
- [ ] $black_pepper = to taste$

## Instructions
1. Place the $chicken_breast$ and 1/4 cup $cornstarch$ in a resealable plastic bag, shake to coat evenly
2. Heat the $vegetable_oil$ in a large pan over high heat
3. Add the $chicken_breast$ to the pan in a single layer and season to taste with $salt$ and $black_pepper$. Cook for 3-4 minutes per side or until browned. Cook in multiple batches if needed
4. Remove the chicken from the pan and place on a plate lined with paper towels
5. Add the $minced_garlic$ and $minced_ginger$ to the pan and cook for 30 seconds
6. Add the $soy_sauce$, $sesame_oil$, $water$ and $brown_sugar$ to the pan and bring to a simmer
7. Mix the 2 teaspoons of $cornstarch$ with 1 tablespoon of cold water. Add the cornstarch mixture to the sauce and bring to a boil; boil for 30-60 seconds until just thickened
8. Add the chicken and $green_onions$ to the pan and toss to coat with the sauce. Serve over rice if desired
