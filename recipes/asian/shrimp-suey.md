# Mongolian Chicken

## Ingredients
### Protein
- [ ] $shrimp = 1 lbs$

### Oil 
- [ ] $vegetable_oil = 2 tbsp$

### Vegetables
- [ ] $carrots = 1$
- [ ] $onion = 1$
- [ ] $cabbage = 1$
- [ ] $zuccini = 1$
- [ ] $mushroom = 1$

### Aromatics
- [ ] $minced_garlic = 1 1/2 tsp$
- [ ] $minced_ginger = 1 tsp$
- [ ] $sesame_oil = 1 tsp, toasted$
- [ ] $sugar = 1/4tsp$
- [ ] $salt = 1/4tsp$

### Sauce
- [ ] $soy_sauce = 3 tbsp$
- [ ] $oyster_sauce = 2 tbsp$
- [ ] $rice_wine = 2 tbsp$
- [ ] $sesame_oil = 1 tsp$
- [ ] $black_pepper = to taste$
- [ ] $water_or_vegetable_broth = 1 cup$
- [ ] $cornstarch = 1 1/2 tbsp$

## Instructions
1. In a bowl, mix together all sauce ingredients and set the sauce aside.
2. Start the rice.
3. Next, preheat the oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add sugar and salt to oil (approx 1/8tsp). Then saut√© the vegetables until cooked.
4. Add half of $rice wine$, sautee for a little longer. Remove from pan.
5. Add oil to pan. Add remaining sugar, salt. Sautee shrimp until golden on both sides. 
6. Add $garlic$, $ginger$, and $rice wine$. Sautee for a little longer. 
7. Give the sauce a quick stir and add it to the pan. Stir until everything is combined and let the sauce simmer until it thickens.
